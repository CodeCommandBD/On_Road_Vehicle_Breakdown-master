# Garage Owner Guide

Welcome to the Garage Partner Dashboard. This guide helps you manage your garage's presence, handle bookings, and grow your business.

## Getting Started

After signing up as a Garage, complete your profile to become visible to users.

### 1. Profile Setup

- Go to **Profile**.
- **Basic Info**: Name, address, phone.
- **Location**: Set your exact location on the map so nearby users can find you.
- **Photos**: Upload professional photos of your workshop.

### 2. Service Management

- Go to **Services**.
- **Add Services**: Select the services you offer (e.g., Towing, Flat Tire, Engine Repair).
- **Pricing**: Set your base price for each service.
- **Availability**: Toggle services on/off based on your current capacity.

### 3. Managing Bookings

- **New Requests**: You will receive notifications for new booking requests.
- **Accept/Reject**: Review user location and problem, then accept or reject the job.
- **In Progress**: Update status as you work on the vehicle.
- **Completion**: Mark as completed and generate an invoice.

### 4. Team Management

- **Add Mechanic**: If you have a team, create accounts for your mechanics.
- **Assign Jobs**: Dispatch mechanics to specific breakdown locations.

### 5. Analytics

- **Performance**: Track your daily/monthly revenue.
- **Reviews**: See what customers are saying about your service.
- **Loyalty**: Manage your membership tier to access premium features.

## Tips for Success

- **Respond Quickly**: Users stuck on the road need immediate help.
- **Keep Profile Updated**: Accurate location and pricing reduce cancellations.
- **Ask for Reviews**: Positive ratings boost your ranking in search results.
